Horse Ride Kufri

Embark on an unforgettable adventure through the rugged trails of Himachal with a thrilling Horse ride Kufri. As one of the most sought-after activities near Shimla, this ride offers the perfect blend of excitement and scenic beauty. The journey takes you up to the majestic Mahasu Peak, the highest point in Kufri, where you are rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the Badrinath and Kedarnath ranges. Whether you are navigating the muddy paths in summer or the snowy trails in winter, a Horse ride Kufri is the quintessential mountain experience for families and adventure seekers alike.
